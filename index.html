Mission

Build a clear, honest, data-driven roadmap for India’s transition to a high-income economy—focused on execution. We avoid vague slogans and prioritize: investment logic, policy design, institution building, and project delivery.

What we publish

  • Infrastructure & rail/metro plans (corridors, timelines, costs)
  • Policy briefs: what to change, why it matters, expected impact
  • Explainers: economics, tradeoffs, and execution bottlenecks
  • Scorecards: progress tracking (simple + transparent metrics)
Open, practical, non-partisan

Who this is for

  • Students and builders who want clarity
  • Professionals working in infrastructure and policy
  • Researchers and analysts
  • Anyone who cares about outcomes, not noise
Community-driven over time

First targets (2025–2030)

These are the “first targets” we’ll publish as detailed, city-by-city / corridor-by-corridor plans. This section will evolve as we add pages and data.

Railways, metros, logistics

  • Identify top 15–20 economic corridors and build a phased plan
  • Prioritize freight reliability + passenger punctuality
  • Metro expansions tied to TOD (transit-oriented development)
  • Station areas as economic zones (walkable, dense, safe)
Where the compounding starts

Manufacturing push

  • Cluster strategy: electronics, auto, chemicals, textiles, defense
  • Export playbooks + logistics cost reduction
  • Simple compliance + faster clearances (time is money)
  • Skill pipelines aligned to clusters
Jobs at scale

Education & research upgrade

  • Research universities: autonomy + funding + accountability
  • More world-class faculty pipelines
  • Industry–academia bridges and labs
  • Outcome-based metrics (not just spend)
Innovation engine

Government execution

  • Project delivery units (PDUs) for mega projects
  • Procurement reform + standardization
  • Transparent dashboards for progress
  • Policy feedback loops (iterate fast)
